ARC Leagues

A structured competitive debate platform for high-performing students — building critical thinking, civic reasoning, and confident voices at scale.

Overview

ARC Leagues is a structured competitive debate platform designed to bring together high-performing students for advanced intellectual engagement. The initiative focuses on developing critical thinking, argumentation, and public speaking skills, creating an environment where participants can engage in meaningful discourse while competing at a high level.

Operating across schools in Gujarat and Rajasthan, ARC Leagues provides a sustained, year-long platform for students to compete, learn, and grow — capped by an annual championship that brings the best debaters together.

Programme Structure

ARC Leagues provides a competitive yet collaborative space where students participate in:

  • Structured British Parliamentary and Asian Parliamentary debate formats
  • Moderated league rounds across the academic year
  • Argument-building and analytical reasoning exercises
  • Peer-based learning and competitive evaluation
  • Annual championship with adjudication by experienced debate coaches

The platform is designed to simulate real-world discourse environments, encouraging participants to think critically and articulate ideas effectively under pressure.

Skill Development Focus

Through consistent participation, students develop:

  • Strong public speaking and communication skills
  • Advanced critical thinking and analytical ability
  • Confidence in presenting and defending complex ideas
  • Exposure to competitive debate ecosystems at regional and national level
  • Structured approaches to research, evidence, and rebuttal

Participant Engagement

The initiative brings together high-performing and motivated students from partner schools, fostering a culture of intellectual curiosity, collaboration, and excellence. ARC Leagues alumni have gone on to represent their schools at national competitions and secure leadership roles in their institutions.

Impact Highlights

  • Competitive debate exposure for students across Gujarat and Rajasthan
  • Structured development of communication and critical thinking skills
  • Multi-school engagement in a sustained league format
  • Pathway to WSDC selection and national debate competitions

Project Outcome

ARC Leagues serves as a foundation for building future leaders, thinkers, and communicators — equipping students with skills that extend beyond debate into academic, professional, and civic spaces.

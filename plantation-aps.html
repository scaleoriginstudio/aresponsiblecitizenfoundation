Environmental Action

Tree Plantation Drive at Army Public School, Ahmedabad

1,000 saplings. One school campus. A student-led model for hands-on environmental education and long-term conservation.

1,000Saplings Planted
APSAhmedabad Cantt
Each OnePlant One Model
SaveSoil Aligned

Overview

ARC Foundation collaborated with Army Public School Ahmedabad Cantt to execute a large-scale tree plantation drive, focused on increasing green cover and promoting environmental responsibility among students. The initiative combined on-ground plantation activity with student engagement, creating a practical learning experience around sustainability and conservation.

Project Execution

The plantation drive was implemented across the school campus with active participation from students, volunteers, and institutional stakeholders. Key components included:

  • Plantation of 1,000 saplings within the campus
  • Student-led participation in planting and nurturing activities
  • Structured coordination to ensure effective coverage across the campus
  • Species selection based on local ecology and climate suitability for long-term survival

Student Engagement & Awareness

The initiative placed strong emphasis on hands-on environmental education, enabling students to directly contribute to sustainability efforts. Participants were encouraged to:

  • Understand the importance of tree plantation and maintaining green cover
  • Take personal ownership of saplings through continued care and monitoring
  • Adopt environmentally responsible practices in their daily lives
  • Connect individual action to collective environmental impact

Each One Plant One — The Model

The project incorporated the Each One Plant One model to ensure long-term impact, encouraging every participant to take responsibility for at least one sapling. Rather than a one-off planting event, this approach creates sustained personal commitment to the survival of each tree planted.

The initiative also aligned with the broader Save Soil movement, reinforcing the importance of ecological conservation and soil health as interconnected environmental priorities.

Environmental Impact

  • 1,000 saplings planted across the Army Public School campus
  • Increased green cover within an institutional environment
  • Active student participation in long-term sustainability initiatives
  • Long-term commitment to plant care and maintenance through the Each One Plant One model

Project Outcome

This initiative demonstrates ARC Foundation's ability to execute high-impact plantation drives within institutional ecosystems, combining environmental action with youth engagement and awareness-building. It serves as a scalable model for integrating education and sustainability through practical, hands-on participation — where students don't just learn about the environment, they become responsible for a part of it.

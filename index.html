Our Conviction
"Responsible citizens don't wait for change — they become it."
Read Our Story

ARC Foundation was born from a simple conviction: the problems India faces — environmental degradation, civic apathy, inequality — can be solved by equipping ordinary people with extraordinary purpose.

We work at the grassroots. Through debate programs that sharpen young minds, plantation drives that restore land, waste management systems that transform villages, and livelihood initiatives that empower women — we build citizens who act.

Since 2016, ARC has pioneered parliamentary debating in Western India, created pathways for students to reach international competitions, reclaimed over 8 acres of degraded land, and helped build women-led enterprises that sustain communities.

Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

Not at all. We welcome anyone who is passionate about the cause. For debate programmes, we run onboarding sessions for new volunteers and adjudicators.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. ARC Foundation is registered under 80G of the Income Tax Act. All donations are eligible for tax deduction and we provide receipts for all contributions.

Can ARC run programmes at our school?

Absolutely. We partner with schools and colleges across Gujarat and Rajasthan to deliver debate workshops, environmental drives, and more. Get in touch using the form above.

Are internships paid?

Internships at ARC are currently unpaid, but offer hands-on experience and a strong reference. We're working toward offering stipends — your donations help make that possible.

Where does ARC operate?

Our primary focus is Western India — Gujarat and Rajasthan — but our debate programmes have connected with students nationwide through online formats like ThinkOff.

Environmental Action

IAF Campus Greening

A plantation drive at the Indian Air Force headquarters in Gandhinagar — integrating environmental sustainability into an institutional wellness programme.

IAFInstitutional Partner
Mar–Apr2023
GreenerCampus Coverage
Yoga &Meditation Context

Overview

ARC Foundation executed a plantation drive at the Indian Air Force headquarters in Gandhinagar during the Yoga and Meditation Workshop held from 22 March to 21 April 2023. The initiative was designed to integrate environmental sustainability with institutional well-being programmes, contributing to increased green cover while promoting collective responsibility toward the environment.

The pairing of environmental action with a wellness programme was deliberate: both share the idea that individual responsibility — toward one's body and toward the natural world — creates stronger institutions and communities.

Project Execution

ARC Foundation led the on-ground implementation of the plantation activity, ensuring structured participation and effective execution within a high-discipline institutional setting. The drive focused on:

  • Organised plantation within the IAF campus grounds
  • Active involvement of programme participants in sustainability efforts
  • Alignment with broader wellness and environmental objectives of the hosting institution
  • Selection of appropriate species for the campus environment and climate

Environmental Impact

The plantation drive contributed to strengthening ecological awareness while delivering measurable environmental benefits within the institutional premises. Key outcomes include:

  • Increased green cover within the IAF campus, Gandhinagar
  • Promotion of environmentally responsible practices within an institutional culture
  • Encouragement of participatory sustainability efforts among programme attendees
  • Long-term contribution to the ecological health of the campus

Institutional Engagement

Conducted within the Indian Air Force ecosystem, the initiative reflects ARC Foundation's ability to collaborate in structured, high-discipline environments and deliver impact-driven sustainability interventions at the institutional level. The IAF's participation signals how environmental responsibility can be embedded in the culture of institutions that shape civic and national life.

Impact Highlights

  • Plantation conducted within the IAF headquarters, Gandhinagar
  • Contribution to increased green cover on institutional premises
  • Engagement of participants in sustainability-focused action alongside a wellness programme
  • Alignment of environmental responsibility with personal and institutional well-being

Project Outcome

This initiative demonstrates how targeted environmental actions can be effectively integrated into institutional programmes, creating a balance between personal well-being and ecological responsibility. It serves as a model for how ARC can partner with large institutions to build green habits alongside other development activities.

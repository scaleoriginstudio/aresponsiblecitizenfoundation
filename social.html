01 — Women's Enterprise

Adhyashakti Flour Mill —
Gandhinagar.

Proof of concept: women-led, community-sustained.

The Adhyashakti Flour Mill is proof of concept: women in rural Gandhinagar, given the right infrastructure and institutional support, built a sustainable enterprise that serves their community.

ARC's role was to help establish the infrastructure, provide initial training, and connect the enterprise to markets — then step back and let the community lead. The mill is now women-owned and women-operated, providing sustainable income to rural households.

  • Developed with DRDA Gandhinagar and MGNF
  • Women-led from operations to financial management
  • Established and serving surrounding rural community
  • Replicable model for rural enterprise development
  • ARC Foundation stepped back — the community continues independently

02 — Education

Education support —
access for all.

Not just fees. A holistic investment in a future.

Access to quality education remains deeply unequal across India. ARC's education support programme directly facilitates the schooling and learning of underprivileged students — covering costs, providing materials, and connecting students to mentors and long-term opportunity.

Each student is supported with a holistic approach: not just fees, but mentorship, resources, and ongoing follow-up. To date, ARC has facilitated the education of 5 students who would otherwise have been unable to continue.

  • 5 students' education directly facilitated
  • 100% continued their schooling
  • Holistic support — fees, materials, mentorship, follow-up
  • Connected to MGNF Livelihood Initiative networks
03 — Community

Community campaigns —
hyper-local, participatory.

Campaigns that respond to real community needs.

ARC runs targeted community campaigns that bring people together around shared goals — health awareness, civic responsibility, sanitation, and more. These are hyper-local, participatory, and designed around the lived experience of community members.

  • Health and hygiene awareness drives with NHM
  • Civic responsibility campaigns with partner schools
  • COVID-19 relief — oxygen concentrators, ration kits, medicines
  • Sashakt partnership for community empowerment
  • Village Waste Management — infrastructure and training

Our Approach

We build capacity,
not dependency.

Method

Community-Led

Every programme is designed with community input. We listen before we act, centre the voices of those we serve, and design solutions that work for the people — not just the funder.

Measurement

Outcome-Focused

We track what matters — households reached, students supported, enterprises sustained — not just activities conducted or awareness raised.

Timeline

Long-Term Thinking

Short-term relief has its place, but sustainable change requires long-term relationships. ARC stays engaged, revisits sites, and iterates based on what we find.

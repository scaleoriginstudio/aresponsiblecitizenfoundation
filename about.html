About ARC Foundation

A Responsible
Citizen, since 2016.

ARC Foundation was built on one question: what if every citizen took responsibility for the world around them? Everything we do flows from that idea.

Our Story

We've always been
driven to give back.

ARC Foundation was founded with a clear purpose: to use privilege, knowledge, and access to create real, lasting change in communities across Western India.

When we started in 2016, parliamentary debating at the school level was largely inaccessible in Gujarat and Rajasthan. We built the infrastructure — the selections, the workshops, the feedback loops — to change that. What began as a regional initiative has since helped create a broader national ecosystem of structured school debating.

Alongside debate, we have reclaimed over 8 acres of degraded land, planted 1 lakh+ trees, reached 300+ households with waste management programmes, and helped build women-led enterprises that sustain communities.

What Drives Us

Our values

01

Critical Thinking First

The ability to think carefully, question assumptions, and form well-reasoned views is the foundation of effective citizenship. Everything we do builds this capacity.

02

Action Over Awareness

We don't just raise awareness — we build systems, run programmes, and measure outcomes. Every ARC initiative is designed for real-world, measurable impact.

03

Community & Inclusion

We work across geographies, schools, and backgrounds — ensuring geography or resources don't limit a young person's access to opportunity.

04

Confident Communication

We train students not just to have opinions, but to articulate them with clarity, evidence, and conviction in any room they walk into.

05

Sustainable Responsibility

Our environmental programmes create lasting, measurable change — not one-off events. We track acres reclaimed, trees planted, and households reached.

06

Youth-Led, Always

ARC is built by and for young people. We believe the next generation doesn't need to wait — they are ready to lead right now.

Our Work

Three verticals.
One vision.

Debate & Speaking

Building minds that argue clearly.

WSDC selections, ARC Leagues, Saturday Spars, ThinkOff, and intensive workshops — a complete ecosystem for school parliamentary debating since 2016.

 Environment

Land restored. Waste reclaimed.

Tree plantations, solid waste management, and land reclamation — turning environmental awareness into measurable, community-sustained action. Every programme begins as a pilot at home before it reaches the community.

 Social Upliftment

Livelihoods built. Communities changed.

Women-led microenterprises, education support, and civic responsibility programmes that build genuine self-reliance from the ground up.

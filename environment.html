01 — Flagship Initiative

ARC Walks & Land
Reclamation.

What started as clean-up walks became something bigger.

What started as community clean-up walks became a systematic village-level waste management operation — and then something unexpected: 8 acres of degraded land brought back to life using repurposed foundry sand.

ARC's land reclamation programme focuses on restoring ecosystems damaged by encroachment, waste dumping, or industrial activity. Each project is community-led — ARC builds the infrastructure and trains the community, then steps back.

  • Village adoption model in Pratapgarh and Ramnagar
  • Waste segregation, collection, and recycling systems installed
  • 8 acres of erosion-damaged land reclaimed and restored
  • Community-led maintenance after ARC Foundation's exit
  • Partnership with AMC and local municipal bodies

02 — Afforestation

Tree Plantation —
1 lakh and counting.

Planting trees is only the beginning.

ARC's tree plantation drives have taken root across Western India — from urban schoolyards to open land that once lay barren. Every drive is student-led and community-anchored, with follow-up visits to ensure survival.

Our most recent milestone: 1,000 saplings planted at Army Public School, Ahmedabad — where every student took personal responsibility for a tree.

  • Over 1,00,000 trees planted across Gujarat and Rajasthan
  • Student-led "Each One Plant One" drives at partner schools
  • 1,000 saplings at Army Public School, Ahmedabad
  • Species selected for local ecology and long-term survival
  • Greening initiative with the Indian Air Force, Gandhinagar

03 — Community Programme

Solid Waste
Management.

Behaviour change, not just awareness.

Household-level solid waste management is one of the most impactful — and most ignored — areas of environmental action. ARC runs targeted campaigns that educate and equip households to segregate, reduce, and responsibly dispose of waste.

We've reached 300+ households, working closely with local government bodies and community leaders to create lasting behavioural change — not just one-off awareness events.

  • 300+ households engaged across multiple neighbourhoods
  • Waste segregation training and tool distribution
  • Partnerships with AMC and local municipal bodies
  • Ongoing monitoring and community feedback loops
  • Infrastructure installed for self-sustaining waste management
Flagship Event

Shilp Aarambh — Official Recycling Partner

ARC served as Official Recycling Partner at the Shilp Marathon for two consecutive years. 12,000+ bottles collected, 80 kg of plastic diverted from landfill, and 20,000+ citizens engaged in a public sustainability pledge — with a Reverse Vending Machine processing plastic in real time.

Featured Event

Walk with a Purpose —
1st February 2020.

Twenty-five young people. One morning. Real change.

On 1st February 2020, twenty-five young volunteers gathered outside Silver Oak University, Gota at 7am to initiate Walk with a Purpose — ARC's contribution to the Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan.

Working alongside Retired Wing Commander Dharmendra Sharma, volunteers used specially engineered collection tools to gather waste without direct contact. Together, they cleared the ground opposite Silver Oak University.

  • 10 full bags of waste collected, including Vimal packets and plastic items
  • Waste disposed with support from AMC trucks
  • Called on manufacturers to take responsibility for post-consumer waste
  • Community participation from passersby — masks and refreshments shared
